01 // Who We Are

Jay's Network operates digital products including the Nexus Coms secure communication platform and associated websites. When this policy refers to "we", "us", or "our", it means Jay's Network and its products.

Our website is jays-network.com. For questions about this policy, contact us at the details listed at the bottom of this page.

02 // Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide and improve our services. This includes:

  • Account Information — name, email address, username, and encrypted password when you create an account.
  • Communications Data — messages, group conversations, and call logs within the Nexus Coms platform.
  • Location Data — GPS coordinates when you actively enable live location sharing or emergency alerts (never collected passively without consent).
  • Device Information — device type, operating system, push notification token, and app version for notification delivery and diagnostics.
  • Usage Data — app interactions, feature usage, and crash reports to improve performance.
  • Profile Data — profile photo and display preferences you choose to provide.

03 // How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate and improve our services:

  • Deliver real-time messaging, group communication, and emergency alerts.
  • Authenticate your identity and maintain secure sessions.
  • Send push notifications for messages, alerts, and important updates.
  • Share your location with authorised group members when you explicitly activate location sharing.
  • Diagnose crashes and technical issues to improve app stability.
  • Respond to support requests and account queries.

>> SECURE_PROMISE: We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for marketing purposes.

05 // Location Data

Location access is entirely optional and user-initiated. We access your location only when:

  • You activate the Live Location Sharing feature within a group.
  • You trigger or receive an Emergency Alert that requires location context.
  • You grant background location permission for active monitoring sessions.

Location data is shared only with members of the specific group you have chosen to share it with. It is not stored permanently — live location data is overwritten on each update and deleted when the sharing session ends.

06 // Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure infrastructure operated by Supabase, with the following protections in place:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plaintext passwords.
  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS and WSS connections.
  • Authentication tokens expire after 7 days and are stored securely on your device.
  • Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access data they are authorised to see.
  • Security headers (HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options) are enforced on all API endpoints.
  • Rate limiting is applied to authentication endpoints to prevent brute force attacks.

07 // Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion:

  • Your profile and account data will be deleted within 30 days.
  • Message history may be retained in anonymised form for up to 90 days before permanent deletion.
  • Live location data is deleted immediately when a sharing session ends.
  • Device push tokens are removed upon logout or account deletion.

08 // Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • The right to request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • The right to withdraw consent for location tracking at any time.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

09 // Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10 // Cookies & Tracking

Our websites may use minimal cookies for session management and security. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or behavioural analytics tools. We do not participate in cross-site tracking.
